What does the determinant tell us?

  • Magnitude: Volume scaling factor of the transformation
  • Sign: Indicates orientation (positive = preserves, negative = reverses)
  • Zero: Collapses space to lower dimension (singular/non-invertible)
  • Non-zero: Invertible transformation

Key Properties

  • $\det(I) = 1$ (Identity matrix)
  • $\det(AB) = \det(A) \times \det(B)$ (Multiplicative property)
  • $\det(A^T) = \det(A)$ (Transpose property)
  • $\det(A^{-1}) = \frac{1}{\det(A)}$ (if $\det(A) \neq 0$)
  • $\det(kA) = k^n \det(A)$ for $n \times n$ matrix
